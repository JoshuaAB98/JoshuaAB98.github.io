Eat Less Meat

Meat is like a black hole for resources when it comes to keeping the animals alive for consumption. Meat and dairy production use 27% of the global freshwater consumption. Of the plants we feed to cows, only 4% of the protein and 3% of the calories is converted into the meat we eat. Resulting in a loss of 97% of potential calories to feed ourselves. This is potentially world hunger ending.

Plan Your Meals

Reducing waste by not buying unnecessary food items can help to reduce demand for items, such as meat, and therefore reduce the supply. By simply planning meals out in advance and creating a list of the ingredients that are required we can avoid buying unneeded items that may be thrown away. This will also result in saving money on food, nobody likes to throw money away.
